## üéØ **What Are Google Sitelinks?**

**Google Sitelinks** are the additional links Google automatically shows below your main search result when:

1. **Your site has authority** - Google trusts your website
2. **Your site has clear structure** - Well-organized navigation
3. **Your site has traffic** - People search for your brand and click
4. **Your site has engagement** - Low bounce rate, high time-on-site
5. **Your brand has search volume** - People actively search "Astro Hema"

**Important:** You CANNOT directly control sitelinks. Google generates them automatically based on your website's structure, popularity, and user behavior.
