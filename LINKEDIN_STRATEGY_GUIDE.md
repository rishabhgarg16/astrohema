- Astrology basics and concepts
- How to read your birth chart
- Understanding planets and houses
- Zodiac sign characteristics
- Vastu principles for home/office

**Pillar 2: Daily Predictions (25%)**
- Daily horoscopes
- Weekly forecasts
- Monthly predictions
- Festival/eclipse updates
- Planetary transit insights

**Pillar 3: Client Success Stories (15%)**
- Testimonials (with permission)
- Before/after transformation stories
- Problem-solving case studies
- Anonymous client experiences

**Pillar 4: Practical Tips & Remedies (15%)**
- Simple home remedies
- Gemstone recommendations
- Vastu quick fixes
- Mantras and rituals
- Dos and don'ts for each zodiac

**Pillar 5: Promotional Content (5%)**
- Course announcements
- Special offers
- Webinar invitations
- Free consultation days
- New service launches

### 3.2 Post Types & Frequency

**Daily Posts (Monday - Saturday):**
- 1 post per day
- Best times: 8-9 AM or 6-7 PM IST
- Mix different formats throughout the week

**Post Format Distribution:**
- 40% - Carousel posts (educational)
- 25% - Image posts with tips
- 20% - Text-only insights
- 10% - Video content
- 5% - Polls/questions

## 9. Analytics & Optimization

### 9.1 Key Metrics to Track:

**Weekly:**
- Post impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Profile views
- New followers
- Click-through rate to website

**Monthly:**
- Total reach
- Best performing content type
- Best performing topics
- Audience demographics
- Conversion rate (leads/bookings)

### 9.2 LinkedIn Analytics Dashboard:

**Access Analytics:**
1. Personal Profile: Click "Analytics" in your profile
2. Company Page: Click "Analytics" tab

**What to Look For:**
- Which posts got the most engagement?
- What time/day performs best?
- Which content format works best?
- Who is your audience? (job titles, industries, locations)

### 9.3 Optimization Checklist:

**Every Week:**
- [ ] Identify top 3 performing posts
- [ ] Analyze what made them successful
- [ ] Create similar content for next week
- [ ] Note which hashtags performed best
- [ ] Check profile views trend

**Every Month:**
- [ ] Review content pillar performance
- [ ] Adjust content mix based on data
- [ ] Update profile/page if needed
- [ ] Archive or delete poor-performing content
- [ ] Set goals for next month

**Every Quarter:**
- [ ] Comprehensive analytics review
- [ ] Survey followers for content preferences
- [ ] Update strategy based on insights
- [ ] Refresh visual templates
- [ ] Evaluate ROI (leads generated, bookings)
